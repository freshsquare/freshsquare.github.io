  1. Discovery & Research

    First we find out what you want
    then research & analyze how to make your vision a reality

    We start every project by reviewing and analyzing your wants, industry competitors, relevant projects and suitable references.

    Once all information is acquired and we deem our research to be completed, we explore possible concepts.

  2. Initial Concept & Design

    We create concepts and you choose what you like
    then we develop

    Once a decision is made in regards to direction, we refine our original concept and discuss with the client details.

    Development begins and we bring our concept to life. During creation we may ask for personal preferences and verify product satisfaction.

  3. Development & Launch

    Finally, we reveal our product
    and watch as your smile grow

    The final solution will be delivered to the client as per deadline requirements. We like to then nurture our product by offering maintenance and support if needed.

    Our goal is satisfaction.
